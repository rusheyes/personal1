In the office halls, a light so bright,
Jovelyn's laughter, a joyful sight.
A jolly heart, a spirit free,
In every moment, sharing glee.
With health and vigor, she'd embrace,
A shining presence in this place.
Outgoing soul, like sun's warm ray,
Spreading cheer both night and day.
Now time has come for her to part,
But memories linger, in every heart.
Farewell, dear Jovelyn, with spirits high,
As you spread your wings, touch the sky.
May your journey onward be,
As vibrant and lively as the sea.
Though you're leaving, remember this:
A cherished friend, you'll always be amiss.
So here's to you, with love so true,
Jovelyn, our co-worker, adieu.
